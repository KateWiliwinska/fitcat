Fitcat fitness studio

Fitcat is the brainchild of PT & Fitness Instructor Sofia Zolobova, who's worked at London's top gyms including Gymbox, Pure Fitness, Better and FRAME for over 12 years.

We're experts in bodyweight strength training, mobility and flexibility, offering personal training and regular group classes and workshops, both in London and online.

We believe in making fitness fun and accessible for anyone. We love working with dancers, sportspeople, yogis and fitness enthusiasts of all skill levels.

If you're bored of bland classes and you're ready to up your fitness game, you've found the right place!

We're the new cool cats on the block, here to shake up London's fitness scene.
